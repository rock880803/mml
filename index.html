Introduction

In recent years, the world is facing an aging population and the population of chronic diseases is gradually rising, resulting in a serious financial burden on national health care. As we age, the human skeleton ages with us. Bone defects caused by cancer, trauma, infection, genetic deformity, or tumor are among the symptoms that also appear in the skeleton, and to avoid ineffective treatment or waste of medical resources, the treatment of these conditions is of great concern2. Osteomyelitis caused by bacterial infection is the main complication in common bone reconstruction surgery. The conventional treatment methods include surgery, chemotherapy, and radiotherapy3. However, the clinical satisfaction of these treatments can be improved.

根據2022年，聯合國的數據顯示，全球65歲以上人口的增長速度超越年輕群體。於2050年，每6人中將有一人在65歲以上(16%)，因此全球正面臨人口老化的困境。而隨著年齡增長，人類的骨骼也會日漸老化，同樣出生於骨骼的症狀亦有癌細胞、創傷、感染或腫瘤所導致的骨缺損等，為避免無效治療或醫療資源的浪費，如何治療以上病症在目前醫療界獲得極大的關注度2。常見的骨重建手術中，因細菌感染引起的骨髓炎為主要併發症。常規的治療手法有手術、化療、放射療法等3。然而，對於上述療法之臨床滿意程度仍可以改進。

Self-Assembly

The chemical structure of P123 contains a hydrophilic head and a hydrophobic tail, which allows P123 to form an interfacial adsorption between the aqueous and organic phases and to form a stable mesophase. When P123 is added to a bioactive glass precursor, it adsorbs to the surface of the glass precursor and forms an organic-inorganic complex. When this complex is sprayed into a high temperature and humidity environment, water evaporates from the organic phase, causing the organic phase to concentrate and form a P123-coated bioactive glass particle. These particles are heat treated (calcined at high temperature) to remove the organic phase, resulting in a bioactive glass with a mesoporous structure.

P123的化學結構包含親水基（hydrophilic head）和疏水基（hydrophobic tail），這使得P123能夠在水和有機相之間形成介面吸附，並形成穩定的介面膜。當P123被添加到生物活性玻璃的前驅體中時，它會吸附在玻璃前驅體的表面，並形成一個有機-無機複合體。當這種複合體被噴霧到高溫高濕的環境中時，水分從有機相中蒸發，使得有機相濃縮，進而形成一個P123包覆的生物活性玻璃微粒。這些微粒通過熱處理（高溫煆燒）以去除有機相，最終形成具有介孔結構的生物活性玻璃

  • In vitro: 細胞最佳生存環境為pH7.2-7.6，但目前並無明顯發現各樣品間的區別
