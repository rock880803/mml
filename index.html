Introduction

In recent years, the world is facing an aging population and the population of chronic diseases is gradually rising, resulting in a serious financial burden on national health care. As we age, the human skeleton ages with us. Bone defects caused by cancer, trauma, infection, genetic deformity, or tumor are among the symptoms that also appear in the skeleton, and to avoid ineffective treatment or waste of medical resources, the treatment of these conditions is of great concern2. Osteomyelitis caused by bacterial infection is the main complication in common bone reconstruction surgery. The conventional treatment methods include surgery, chemotherapy, and radiotherapy3. However, the clinical satisfaction of these treatments can be improved.

根據2022年，聯合國的數據顯示，全球65歲以上人口的增長速度超越年輕群體。於2050年，每6人中將有一人在65歲以上(16%)，因此全球正面臨人口老化的困境。而隨著年齡增長，人類的骨骼也會日漸老化，同樣出生於骨骼的症狀亦有癌細胞、創傷、感染或腫瘤所導致的骨缺損等，為避免無效治療或醫療資源的浪費，如何治療以上病症在目前醫療界獲得極大的關注度2。常見的骨重建手術中，因細菌感染引起的骨髓炎為主要併發症。常規的治療手法有手術、化療、放射療法等3。然而，對於上述療法之臨床滿意程度仍可以改進。

EISA (Evaporation-induced self-assembly)

Self assembly is usually defined as "the spontaneous organization of data through non covalent interactions (such as hydrogen bonding, van der Waals force, electrostatic force, π – π interaction, etc.) without external intervention". In the process of self-assembly, asymmetric molecules (the most common amphiphilic surfactants) are usually spliced and pre programmed to assemble ordered supramolecular structures. After contacting with the aqueous solution, the surfactant molecule is self-organized, exposing the hydrophilic part to the surrounding medium, and masking the hydrophobic part inside the micelle.

自組裝通常被定義為“資料通過非共價相互作用（如氫鍵、范德華力、靜電力、π–π相互作用等）自發組織，無需外部干預”。在自組裝過程中，不對稱分子（最常見的兩親性表面活性劑）通常被接合併預先程式設計以組裝成有序的超分子結構。與水溶液接觸後，表面活性劑分子自發自組織，將親水部分暴露於周圍介質，同時將疏水部分遮罩在膠束內部。
